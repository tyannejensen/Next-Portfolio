## Installation

enough chatter-lets get it running on your machine!

### Step 1: Create a New Next.js app

Fire up your terminal and run:

```
npx create-next-app my-app
```

This sets up everything you need. It will ask you a few questions, i.e. do you
want TypeScript, eslint, and some other packages. I typically say yes to most of
them. Find your preferences.

### Step 2: Navigate to & Start Your Project

```
cd my-app
npm run dev
```

This launches the dev server, and just like that, you are looking at your
